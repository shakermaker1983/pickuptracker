Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Pickups is a free Android app that counts how many times you pick up your phone each day. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains clearly what data the app does and does not collect.

Data we collect

Pickups does not collect any personal data. The app tracks the number of times your screen is unlocked each day. This count is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted anywhere.

Data we share

We do not share any data with third parties. Ever. There are no analytics, no advertising networks, and no third-party SDKs in the app.

Internet access

Pickups does not connect to the internet. The only exception is the optional Buy Me a Coffee link, which opens your browser if you choose to tap it. No data is sent — it simply opens a webpage.

Data storage

All data — including your daily pickup count, your daily goal, and your historical averages — is stored entirely on your device using Android's built-in storage. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all of this data.

Children

Pickups does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page. Since the app collects no data, we do not anticipate any meaningful changes.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at the email address listed on our Google Play store listing.
